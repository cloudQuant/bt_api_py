# Algo Fee Structure

> 来源: [Binance Algo Trading API](https://developers.binance.com/docs/algo/spot-algo)

## Fee Rate

Fees charged may vary depending on the algorithmic strategies selected. The fee schedule is as follows:

| VIP Level | Fee Rate |
|-----------|----------|
| Regular users and users up to VIP 4 | FDUSD/USDT, USDC/USDT: 0%; BTC/USDT, BTC/USDC, BTC/FDUSD, ETH/USDT, ETH/USDC, ETH/FDUSD: 0.05%; All Other Pairs: 0.12% |
| VIP 5 and above | All listed Pairs: 0.00% |
